"Algebras/MFE/alg_pknot_mfe.gap" is the same as Algebras/MFE/alg_mfe.gap, but extended to algebra-functions for pseudoknots.

Since some algebra functions are commonly used by other algebras (Algebras/MFE/alg_mfe.gap and Algebras/MFE/alg_outside_mfe.gap), they are outsourced in the files Algebras/MFE/algpart_mfe_basic.gap.
