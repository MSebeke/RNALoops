Jiabin Huang defined three level of position specific shapes, called helix center shapes (hishapes). The center of a helix is the middle position of the unpaired loop region. For odd regions, the position can be x.5. alg_hishape_h accounts only for centers in hairpin loops. alg_hishape_m records centers for multiloops in addition to the hairpin loop centers. alg_hishape_b extends alg_hishape_m by also recording centers for bulges and internal loops.
